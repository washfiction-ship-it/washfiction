Common questions

FAQ

Everything you need to know before booking.

How often should I pressure wash my driveway in Florida?

Florida's heat and humidity accelerate algae, mold, and mildew growth on concrete surfaces. Most homeowners in Port Saint Lucie benefit from having their driveway cleaned once or twice a year. If your driveway stays shaded or wet it may need more frequent cleaning.

Is soft washing safe for stucco and painted surfaces?

Yes — soft washing uses low pressure and a professional cleaning solution to safely remove mold and algae from stucco, vinyl, Hardie board, and painted surfaces without causing damage. High pressure washing on these surfaces can crack stucco, strip paint, and force water behind siding. We always use the correct method for your home's exterior material.

How much does pressure washing cost in Port Saint Lucie?

Wash Fiction LLC prices by square footage so every customer pays a fair rate based on their home size. House washing starts at $0.25 per square foot with a $300 minimum. Driveway cleaning starts at $0.20 per square foot with a $150 minimum. Most PSL homes run $300–$600 for a house wash and $150–$250 for a driveway.

How do I remove algae from my pool deck?

Pool decks in Florida collect algae, mildew, and organic growth quickly due to constant moisture and heat. We use a commercial surface cleaner at the correct pressure for your deck material — concrete, pavers, cool deck, or travertine — to safely remove all buildup without damaging the surface or affecting your pool chemistry.

Do I need to be home when you clean?

Not necessarily. As long as we have access to an outdoor water spigot and any gate codes needed to reach the areas being cleaned, you don't need to be present. We send before and after photos to your phone when the job is complete.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Wash Fiction LLC carries $1,000,000 general liability insurance and holds active business tax receipts with both the City of Port Saint Lucie and St. Lucie County. We can provide a certificate of insurance upon request.
